About event

This page lists all registered participants of the MDS26 EUDI Wallet interoperability testing network. Each entry includes the organisation's test endpoints and public root certificates needed to establish trust during testing sessions.

Before the event: download the root certificates of every organisation you plan to test with and pre-load them into your wallet, issuer, or verifier. This is required to validate credential exchanges during the interoperability sessions.

Participants: if your endpoints or certificates are missing or outdated, please update your entry by sending the information to wallet@egov.md.

Data formats

Participants support ISO 18013-5 (mdoc) and/or SD-JWT VC — the two core EUDI Wallet credential formats.

Presentation modes

Remote (online/network) and proximity (NFC/Bluetooth) presentation flows are covered across the network.

Certificates

Public root certificates for Wallet Providers, Issuers, and Verifiers are available to download or copy per organisation below.
