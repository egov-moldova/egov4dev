## Prerequisites:  

A beneficiary, requesting integration with MNotify, shall meet the following conditions conditions: 

1. To own an information system to be integrated with MNotify; 
2. For public beneficiaries – the system shall be registered with the [Registrul Resurselor și Sistemelor Informaționale](https://rsi.gov.md/procedure) (Recommended);  
3. To have concluded a Contract and/or Annex to use MNotify service; 
4. For private beneficiaries – this service shall be paid according to the contract concluded; 
5. To obtain the system certificate from STISC; 
6. To configure the system certificate in MPass. 
 
## Connection steps for the beneficiary: 

1. To fill in the Formularul unic de conectare;  
2. To sign the contract and/or annex for providing the governmental electronic notification service (MNotify) 
3. To obtain the system certificate from [STISC](https://semnatura.md/order/system-certificate). 
4. To provide EGA with the file .cer (public key) by email at [servicii@egov.md](servicii@egov.md) or the administrator shall configure the public key for the necessary service in MPass;  
5. To fill in the integration data in the [Form](https://forms.office.com/Pages/DesignPageV2.aspx?origin=NeoPortalPage&subpage=design&id=Z4f8jWsRaEKDxfvIWTRtODb3-31Q6N1Hrz_Ou4oWQqBUNlpMVU02NThQU1oxQko5T0I1WkVMN0dQSi4u) 
    - Name of information system in MPass; 
    - IP addresses: 
      - for testing environment - Ex. 172.46.45.10 
      - for production environment - Ex. 172.46.45.11 
    - Details regarding the notification channels to be used 
6. EGA configures access on the testing environment; 
7. The beneficiary performs the operational tests in compliance with the Guide; 
8. Upon the beneficiary’s final validation, EGA is to activate the service on the production environment; 
9. The beneficiary shall be notified when the integration completed.
