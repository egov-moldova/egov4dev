## Prerequisities

1. Owns an information system intended for integration with MSign;
2. For public beneficiaries – the system is registered in the Registrul Resurselor și Sistemelor Informaționale;
3. The contract and/or annex regarding the use of the MSign service is signed;
4. For private beneficiaries – the use of the service is paid according to the contract;
5. Beneficiaries obtain the system certificate from STISC;
6. Users possess an electronic signature for signing through MSign. 

## Connection steps for the beneficiary: 

1. Complete the Unified Connection Form;
2. Sign the contract and annex for the provision of authentication and authorization services through MSign;
3. Obtain the system certificate from STISC;
4. Send to AGE only the .cer file (public key) at servicii@egov.md;
5. Provide the integration details through the form, including: 
   * The name of the information system; 
   * The IP address(es) of the service; 
6. AGE configures access in the test environment: https://mpass.staging.egov.md;
7. The beneficiary performs internal testing;
8. After the beneficiary’s final validation, AGE activates the service in the production environment;
9. The beneficiary receives a notification confirming the completion of the integration.
